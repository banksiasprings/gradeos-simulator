🛰 Free RTK Corrections (NTRIP)

Connects to a free NTRIP caster to pull live RTCM differential corrections — improves position from ±3m raw GPS down to ±30mm effective. rtk2go.com is free with no login required.

Change mountpoint to nearest AUSCORS/rtk2go station for best results. Falls back to EMA simulation if NTRIP unreachable.
